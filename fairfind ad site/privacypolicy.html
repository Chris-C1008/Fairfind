Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2025

Introduction

At FairFind, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, browser extension, and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Information you provide directly, such as your email address when signing up for an account or submitting discount codes.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our website and extension, such as pages visited, clicks, and search queries.
  • Technical Data: Information about the device you use to access our services, including browser type, operating system, and IP address.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Improve our services and personalize your experience.
  • Verify and validate discount codes submitted by the community.
  • Notify you of updates, promotions, and new features.
  • Ensure compliance with our terms of service.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate and improve our platform.
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect our rights and users.
  • Aggregated and anonymized data with partners to improve discount code accuracy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@fairfind.com.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure data storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
